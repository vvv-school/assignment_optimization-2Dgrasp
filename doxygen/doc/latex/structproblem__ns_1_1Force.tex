\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Descriptor of the force acting on the object.

It is given in the form F=$<$t,fn,ft$>$, where\+:
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item t\+: double that identifies the location on the objet\textquotesingle{}s perimeter where F is applied.
\item fn\+: double that specifies the amount of force applied along the direction normal to the object\textquotesingle{}s perimeter; fn$>$0 for inward direction.
\item ft\+: double that specifies the amount of force applied along the direction tangential to the object\textquotesingle{}s perimeter; ft$>$0 for direction along velocity. 
\end{DoxyItemize}
